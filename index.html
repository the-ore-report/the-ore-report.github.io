What it is

The Ore Report is a free monthly newsletter that rounds up the major developments across mining and explains why they matter. Every story is summarized in clear, jargon-free language, with unfamiliar terms defined right where they come up.

It's built for college students and people newer to the mining industry — curious readers who want to follow the field without needing an insider's vocabulary to keep up.

What it covers

Critical Minerals

Supply, demand, pricing, policy, and new projects for the minerals behind batteries, defense, and clean energy — lithium, cobalt, nickel, copper, and rare earths.

LiDAR & Geospatial

Drone surveying, point clouds, photogrammetry, and mapping technology used to survey and monitor mine sites and terrain.

AI & Automation

How artificial intelligence and automation are changing mining — autonomous equipment, predictive maintenance, and AI in exploration, planning, and modeling.

Reclamation & Closure

Restoring mined land: revegetation, soil and water management, closure planning, and the regulations that govern it.
